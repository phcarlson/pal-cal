About PalCal

Our Inspiration

We at UMass Amherst wanted to develop an application that would be genuinely useful for college students like us! So our idea was to provide a web application that allows you to form private groups of friends, add your weekly course schedule, and autogenerate a schedule of the group membersâ€™ mutual free time. This takes away a lot of the stressful back and forth in planning around our busy lives, with a twist of a more student-focused social aspect that other apps lack!

What To Expect

After signing up:

  • Fill out your profile a little!
    • Upload a profile photo to stand out
    • Provide details about your college
    • Add a bio for anything, relevant to planning or not
    • And more to come...

Something that will be emphasized in the future is sharing streaming services you are subscribed to and are willing to share. This way, you could better plan movie/show viewings!

Once your friends are also signed up:

  • Shoot them a request based on their username shared with you
    • Friend requests are found on your profile
    • Being friends allows you to add them as members to a newly created group
    • Begin planning events together in the group you made!
      • You can always invite more friends later
    • After planning events, RSVP so your friends know who is coming

Smooth sailing from here, y'all.
